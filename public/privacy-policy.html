Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

AI Academic Navigator is a cognitive assessment platform developed by the Faculty of Economics and Management, Czech University of Life Sciences Prague, for academic research purposes.

1. Data We Collect

When you use AI Academic Navigator, we may collect:

  • Assessment Data: Your responses to cognitive tests and questionnaires
  • Health Data (optional): If you connect a Garmin device, we access stress levels, heart rate variability (HRV), and heart rate data during assessment sessions
  • Voice Data (optional): Voice interview transcripts (processed locally, not stored on servers)
  • Basic Information: Your name (for personalization purposes only)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized cognitive assessment and learning recommendations
  • To correlate physiological stress data with cognitive performance (research purposes)
  • To generate AI-powered insights about your learning style and study habits
  • For academic research on AI-enhanced education (anonymized and aggregated)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All assessment data is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). No data is sent to our servers.
  • Garmin Data: Health data from Garmin is accessed via secure OAuth2 authentication and processed only during your session.
  • AI Processing: Text prompts (without personal identifiers) are sent to OpenAI for generating personalized recommendations.
  • GDPR Compliance: We follow EU General Data Protection Regulation requirements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • OpenAI (for AI-generated recommendations - anonymized prompts only)
  • Academic publications (only anonymized, aggregated research findings)

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored in your browser's localStorage)
  • Delete your data (clear browser data or use the app's delete function)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability

6. Garmin Health Data

If you connect your Garmin device:

  • We only access data you explicitly authorize via Garmin Connect
  • Health data is used solely to correlate stress with cognitive performance
  • You can disconnect your Garmin account at any time
  • We do not store your Garmin credentials

7. Children's Privacy

This platform is intended for university students (18+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
